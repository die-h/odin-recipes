Hamburger

Description

Classic burger with bread, lettuce, tomato and meat

Ingredients

  • 2 slices of bread
  • 1 Tomato
  • 1 burger
  • A few leaves of lettuce

Steps

  1. Cook the burger
  2. Put the burger over one slice of bread
  3. Add slices of tomato and the lettuce
  4. Put the resting slice of bread over the top and enjoy
