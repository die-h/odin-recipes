Lasagna

Description

A recipe for lasagna, which the most important ingredient is love

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Tomato puree
  • Minced meat
  • Basil

Steps

  1. With milk and flour make the separating part
  2. Cook the minced meat with the tomato puree
  3. Add one layer of the mead separators, add meat with puree
  4. Repeat last step till you run out of ingredients
