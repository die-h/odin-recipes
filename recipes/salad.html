Salad

Description

A recipe of salad for lazy people

Ingredients

  • Lettuce
  • Tomato
  • Onion (optional)
  • Oil, salt and pepper

Steps

  1. Chopp the lettuce and tomato. Also a bit of onion if you want
  2. Put everything in a bowl
  3. Add 2 spoons of vegetable oil
  4. Add salt and pepper to taste
